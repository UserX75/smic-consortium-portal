Minutes 3–5 – Key Features (Demo highlights)
1. Shareholder & Board Management (30 sec)

“You can see all shareholders, their ownership percentage, and board members – with term dates and committee memberships. One source of truth.”

2. Committee Dashboards (1 min)

“Each committee gets its own view. For example, the Investment Committee sees active deals and portfolio performance. The Audit & Risk Committee sees risk metrics. The AGM Electoral Committee manages polls.”

3. Live Voting Polls (1 min)

“When it’s time for AGM elections or budget approval, the committee creates a poll. Members vote instantly, and results update live – no more paper ballots or manual counting.”

4. Investment Reports + AI Analysis (1.5 min)

“This is where AI transforms your decision‑making. The platform automatically analyses news headlines about your investments using a financial sentiment model (FinBERT).
For example, if there’s positive news about Vodacom Lesotho, the system flags it and recommends ‘Consider increasing exposure’. Negative news triggers a review alert.”

“You can also generate quarterly investment reports with AI‑written summaries and recommendations – saving your team days of manual work.”

Minute 6 – Automation & Efficiency Gains
“Beyond AI, automation saves you significant time.”

Examples:

Director fees – quarterly payments are calculated automatically; reminders sent via email.

Capital calls – members are notified when a call is due; the finance team sees a forecast chart.

Regulation updates – new policies can be uploaded and version‑controlled, with notifications to relevant committees.

Meeting attendance – RSVP and online meeting links reduce administrative overhead.

“What used to take your company secretary and finance team days now happens in minutes.”

Minute 7 – Why AI Analysis is a Game‑Changer for the Consortium
“You manage over R200 million in assets across 7 entities. Market sentiment moves fast. AI gives you an edge.”

Three concrete benefits:

Proactive risk management – The AI monitors news and alerts you to negative sentiment about any investment before the market reacts.

Unbiased recommendations – The system analyses data, not politics. It suggests rebalancing when a holding becomes too concentrated (e.g., Sekhametsi Place at 100% ownership).

Faster due diligence – When a new deal appears, the AI can summarise risks and opportunities from thousands of documents in seconds.

“Without AI, you rely on human intuition and delayed reports. With AI, you get real‑time, data‑driven insights.”
